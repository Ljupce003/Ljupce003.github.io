About Me

Hi! I'm Ljupcho Angelovski, a Computer Science student at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI) in Skopje, North Macedonia.

The Philosophy

I'm passionate about writing clean, maintainable code and building things that are actually useful. I enjoy working across the stack, but my core motivation is curiosity. I don't just want to use tools. I want to understand the invisible architecture underneath them. Whether I'm designing algorithms, working with databases, or configuring infrastructure, I need to know exactly how things work under the hood.

The Process

I am a perfectionist with a sharp attention to detail. I actively seek out challenging tasks because the hard stuff is what makes us better developers. When I'm invested in a problem, I will often forget to take breaks until it's solved. I rarely give up on an issue—if something is proving incredibly difficult, I simply step away to make a macchiato or a Nescafé 3in1, and then I get right back to it.

The People

The team is just as important as the tech. I thrive when working alongside bright, driven teammates. I try to bring a gentle, grounded approach to my work, and I am a strict believer in fairness and collaboration.
